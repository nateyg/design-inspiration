### Linear: Dark SaaS Navigation & Hero
*2026-06-23 · Landing page</category>
<parameter name="mood">["minimal", "premium", "tech-forward", "dark", "focused"] · [source](https://linear.app)*

Linear's landing page header demonstrates minimal dark-mode sophistication with a clean horizontal navigation, geometric wordmark, and stark black hero background. The approach prioritizes clarity and premium positioning through extreme whitespace and geometric precision.

- **Palette:** #000000, #FFFFFF, #F5F5F5
- **Type:** Clean sans-serif (appears to be system font or similar minimal stack). Navigation uses regular weight ~14-16px. "Sign up" button uses bold weight on white background.
- **Layout:** Fixed horizontal navigation with symmetric spacing; logo flush-left, navigation menu center-distributed, auth buttons right-aligned. Hero section occupies full-bleed black viewport with ample breathing room. Very generous padding and whitespace creates a "less is more" hierarchy.
- **Take:**
  - Use full-bleed black hero with ample whitespace to signal premium positioning and reduce cognitive load
  - Right-align authentication CTAs while keeping primary nav centered—creates visual balance without clutter
  - Pair minimal geometric wordmark with generous padding in nav bar (~20-32px) to avoid cramped feeling
  - Let negative space work harder than content; the emptiness itself conveys confidence and clarity
  - Fixed nav should maintain consistent vertical padding (appears ~16-20px) to feel anchored but not heavy

### Neon pixelated grid chaos
*2026-06-24 · Mobile UI · image*

A vibrant y2k-inspired feed interface layered with a high-contrast monochrome figure, creating visual tension between chaotic neon color blocking and stark geometric forms. The grid-based layout feels both playful and glitchy.

- **Palette:** #FF00FF, #00FFFF, #00FF00, #FFFF00, #FF0080, #00AAFF, #FFFFFF, #000000
- **Type:** Sans-serif UI labels appear minimal and utilitarian in small scale; focus is on visual elements over typographic hierarchy.
- **Layout:** Dense grid-based composition with small square modules (pixel blocks) arranged in irregular clusters. Heavy use of white space breaks between blocks creates visual rhythm. Figure positioned off-center with layered depth through contrast.
- **Mood:** playful, chaotic, retro, glitchy, y2k, energetic
- **Take:**
  - Use neon color blocking in discrete geometric units to create visual interest without symmetry
  - Layer high-contrast monochrome figures over busy backgrounds to add focal points and graphic punch
  - Grid-based layouts feel modular—allow uneven clustering rather than rigid alignment for dynamic energy
  - Pair bright complementary colors (magenta + cyan, yellow + purple) for that y2k pop
  - Small UI affordances (icons, buttons) at bottom don't need to match the chaos—understated nav creates breathing room

`#pixelated #neon #y2k #grid-layout #mobile-feed #color-blocking #contrast #chaotic-composition #retro-futurism`
